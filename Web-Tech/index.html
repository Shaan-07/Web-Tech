Cloud Computing

This website highlights the crucial role of green cloud computing and its impact on data management. Energy consumption of datacentres across the globe is pushing the need for a more energy efficient ways to manage data such as green cloud computing. Green Cloud computing is a new way to approach the problem and has emerged as a crucial strategy to achieve the goal of net-zero future. Organizations can reduce their carbon footprint and energy consumption significantly by adopting green cloud computing for data storage, transmission and processing. As data plays an important role in modern business operations, the environmental impact of data processing becomes a major concern.
